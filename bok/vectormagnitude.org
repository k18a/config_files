#+TITLE: Vector Length
- length of a vector [a] is given as
- it is basically the square root of the vector's [[file:vectordotproduct.org][dot product]] with itself
\begin{equation}
\begin{bmatrix}
a_1\\
\vdots \\
a_n
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
||[a]|| = \sqrt(a_1 + \dots + a_n) = \sqrt([a].[a])
\end{equation}
