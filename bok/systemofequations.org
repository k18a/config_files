#+TITLE: System of Equations

- general form
\begin{equation}
\Sigma _{i=1} ^m
a_{i1}x_1 + \dots + a_{in}x_n = b_i
\end{equation}
- matrix form
\begin{equation}
\begin{bmatrix}
a_{11} & \dots & a_{1n} \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
a_{m1} & \dots & a_{mn} 
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x_1 \\
\vdots \\
x_n
\end{bmatrix}
= 
\begin{bmatrix}
b_1 \\
\vdots \\
b_m
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
