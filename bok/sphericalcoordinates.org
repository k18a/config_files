#+TITLE: Spherical Co-ordinates

- in terms of cartesian co-ordinates
\begin{equation}
r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}

\theta = tan^{-1} (y/x)

\phi = cos^{-1} (z/r)
\end{equation}
- to go back to cartesioan cordinates
\begin{equation}
x = r cos \theta sin \phi

y = r sin \theta sin \phi

z = r cos \theta
\end{equation}
